Our Journey

Sehat sang is an E-Commerce Ed-Tech website, designed by the team of engineers from Jaipur's Arya College Of Engineering and IT. We started this website in the fourth year of our college as a project for our dream. We used our web-development skills in this and made this website

What We Do

Our Mission

Our mission is to make india a more educated and aware country about Nutrition. We have a dream to achieve to reach every household so that people can understand the importance of right nutrition for their body.So to achieve that goal we provise nutrition coursesmto the mass of india in a pocket friendly price. We also provide customised healthy meals. What's special in that you ask? Well we make customised diets from the normal household groceries. No fancy ingredients needed.
